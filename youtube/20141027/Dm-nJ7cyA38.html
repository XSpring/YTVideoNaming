Now here in this list i composed my top 5 worst power ranger seasons of all time and why i thought so, and here we go

5. Power Rangers: Jungle Fury - I hated this season because the story on this one wasn't well thought out, the team work, yeah non existent

4. Power Rangers: Mystic Force - When i first watched this season it had this one episode which looked cool and had this alternate dark reality which i thought was a good idea and reminded me of an episode of mighty morphin' but then dropped the ball altogether.

3. Power Rangers: Operation Overdrive - Worst theme song, corny storyline and boring plots...the list is endless - even though they had one of the best costumes

2. Power Rangers: Wild Force - I hated this season with a passion and still do , the team work, storyline, the feel of the show, costumes, everything was wrong with this season

1. Power Rangers: Samurai - Where do i begin as much as i hated "wild force" this season overall had to top the list no doubt. This season had a cast that was very inexperienced in the ways of martial arts, awful CGI and a terrible storyline as well as all the seasons previously mentioned there were so many things wrong with this season
