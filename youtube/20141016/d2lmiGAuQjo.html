Nicky Romero vs Krewella - Legacy (Official Music Video)
iTunes US ► http://smarturl.it/Legacy
Beatport ► http://www.beatport.com/track/legacy-...

The new #7 ranked DJ in the world and Protocol Recordings founder, Nicky Romero, releases the long-awaited official video for "Legacy," his hit collaboration with Krewella, via MTV Buzzworthy. Just before he departs on his Halloween mini tour, Nicky Romero comes through with a captivating visual representation of the evocative song that has become a certified anthem. The "Legacy" video is a powerful story about being there for the people you love.

Directed by Kyle Padilla, Nicky Romero & Krewella's "Legacy" video runs through a love story between a cancer-diagnosed mother and her son who becomes a sort of empathetic vigilante. While the mother struggles with her treatment and its effects, her son is determined to do whatever he can to help her feel better -- even if that means bashing in her co-workers car, amongst other things. Although his actions are slightly misdirected, his genuine concern and determination give her strength to continue on her fight against cancer. With this video, Nicky Romero & Krewella have not only given new life to their song, but they have also helped raise awareness for cancer patients.

Three of the actors who shaved their heads in the video donated the hair to Locks of Love, an organization that makes wigs for children undergoing chemotherapy.
