Uploaded on Jan 17, 2012

The first electronic artist to ever receive a Grammy nomination for Best New Artist, Skrillex worked with the Members of The Doors to create a new Rock track for RE:GENERATION. This music video for "Breakn' A Sweat" features the genre-mashing collaboration as well as footage showing Skrillex at work on the stage and on the road.

Presented by Hyundai Veloster, RE:GENERATION is a documentary film that follows DJ Premier, Pretty Lights, The Crystal Method, Mark Ronson, and Skrillex as they remix, recreate and re-imagine five traditional styles of music. Learn more at youtube.com/veloster and regenerationmusicproject.com.
