Published on Sep 9, 2014

Comedic duo Tim & Eric stopped by to discuss the September 18th premiere of Bedtime Stories, their latest venture on the Adult Swim network, how they connected over a general dislike of film school as well as Erics alleged love of Black women.
Regular contributors to Adult Swim, theyve previously enjoyed success with shows including; Tom Goes to the Mayor, Check It Out! With Dr. Steve Brule and of course the Tim and Eric Awesome Show. Fans of the jokesters will also be happy to know that a sequel to 2012s Tim and Erics Billion Dollar Movie is already in the works.
Known for pushing the envelope, they discussed why Justin Bieber deserves a chance as well as their connection to Jamaican music.
