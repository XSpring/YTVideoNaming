Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike: Tomorrowland 2014 full live-set

As more than 400 artists and producers descend upon Belgium’s Boom for the party of a lifetime at Tomorrowland’s, official festival hosts and reigning kings, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike, have reiterated exactly why they’re deserving of the crown.

With over 360,000 amped-up attendees over the course of two weekends, Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike congregated the masses to set Tomorrowland’s colossal main stage ablaze, with stadium-level pyrotechnics, an influx of special guests and a selection of slickest tracks including the biggest and best names in EDM, House and hip-hop. 80,000 saw Dimitri Vegas & Like Mike’s headline performance, cramming the arena, never before had so many people been counted at the main stage.

Watch the Tomorrowland Kings rip-roar through their impressive roster of releases including ‘Wakanda’, ‘Find Tomorrow’, 'Tremor' and the festival’s official anthem for 2014 ‘Waves’, with fellow dance alumni figureheads Diplo, Martin Garrix and 3 Are Legend’s Steve Aoki, all guest appearing for the celebration.

Premiering new collaborations with the likes of Ummet Ozcan and Afrojack, as well as the new vocal version of the epic Mammoth, witness Dimitri & Mike’s impeccable talent that allows viewers who couldn’t make the party, or were unfortunate enough to miss the Tomorrowland TV stream, to strap-in, jump aboard and prepare for 60 minutes of non-stop energy, power and- of course- anthems to match.
