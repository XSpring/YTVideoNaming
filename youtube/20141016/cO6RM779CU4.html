The Singles Collection: 2001--2011 is a compilation album released by British virtual band Gorillaz on 25 November 2011. The album is a collection of the group's singles released between 2001 and 2011. The album is available in four different editions: standard, deluxe, 12" vinyl and 7" single box set. The years 2001--2011 in title of the compilation are somewhat misleading, seeing as "Tomorrow Comes Today" was first released in 2000, while the newest featuring track, "Doncamatic", was released in 2010.
