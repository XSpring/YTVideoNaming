Published on Oct 13, 2014

Jada Pinkett-Smith sat down with Sway to discuss her thoughts on the public criticism her kids have faced, life with husband Will Smith and her friendship with Tupac Shakur.

An accomplished actress, she also spoke about her role as Fish Mooney on Fox's new fall hit "Gotham."

Here's a fun fact; she's a comic book fan.

Listen Carefully: "Worry about being respected, never worry about being liked...I did not teach my kids to be liked, I taught them to always live by their truth and to always respect others. At the end of the day, my kids have the confidence to be themselves."
