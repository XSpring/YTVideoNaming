Published on Aug 12, 2014

Los Angeles rappers, Dilated Peoples stopped by Sway in the Morning to discuss their new album, Directors of Photography. First, the group started off speaking about the new class of rappers who are also lyrical, like TDE. Additionally, they explained how using independent outlets to promote their music helped them. In addition to speaking about their work with Aloe Blacc, Peoples spoke about the importance of calling out wack rappers and emcees to bite other people's styles. Be sure to purchase Dilated Peoples' new album, Directors of Photography on iTunes today!
