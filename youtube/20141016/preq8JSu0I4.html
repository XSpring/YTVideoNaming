ISTANBUL. - An armed criminal who broke into one of the jewelry shops in Istanbul's Bagcilar district met an unforeseen resistance. The incident was recorded by the security camera.
The masked robber entered the shop pulled out his gun and told the salesman to fill his bag, the Turkish Posta informs. At that time a 12-year old child who was in the shop jumped towards the robber who wasn't expecting that at all. The criminal hesitated tried to free himself from the child and ran away.The police studied the recording by the camera and arrested Mustafa S. who lived near the shop.
