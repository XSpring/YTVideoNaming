Hi People!

I truly love this group so I decided to make a compilation of some of my favourite songs by them. (totally needless to say, they were chosen by personal opinion).

I know there aren't any songs from The Fall, D-Sides, Laika come Home, etc. in this compilation. There are tons of songs that I simply love from Gorillaz, maybe even all of them, but I just can't include them all in one video: it'd make it way too long and unlikely for people to watch it.
I picked 12 songs, 4 from Gorillaz, 4 from Demon Days and 4 from Plastic Beach.

Hope you enjoy!

Some other great songs by them in my opinion: Kids with guns, Dare, White flag, Empire ants, Pirate Jet, Plastic Beach, To binge, On melancholy hill, Faust, Rockit, Revolving Doors, Hillbilly man, Shy-Town, Amarillo, Aspen Forest, Dracula, Spitting out the demons, People, Hong Kong, We are happy landfill.
You should go check those out if you liked the ones that were featured in this video.
