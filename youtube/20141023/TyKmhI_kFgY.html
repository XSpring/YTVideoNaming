Who has ever seen Raman scattered light? Under normal circumstances, Raman scattered light is too weak to be seen by the naked eye. Together with the basics of Raman spectroscopy, this video shows how the Raman spectrum of pure water can be obtained through the use of Nanophoton's Raman microscope.
