Published on Nov 13, 2012

From the 10th Anniversary of Star Trek's cousin, Red Dwarf, I bring you the best bits from a little spin-off section entitled, 'Can't Smeg, Won't Smeg', which is based on the possible predecessor to 'Ready, Steady, Cook!', 'Can't Cook, Won't Cook'.

Ainsley Harriott is still the host, but meets very special guests; Dave Lister and Kryten (2X4B - 523P) for the red table; Arnold A.J. (Smeghead) Rimmer and Duane Dibbley - who replaced Cat. Ainsley is also joined by Kristine Kochanski for judging the food. (Even though she declined profusely!)
