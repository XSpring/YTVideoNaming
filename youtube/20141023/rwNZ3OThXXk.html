Published on Jul 18, 2014

Showing off the newest Warframe, Mirage. I show all four of her powers as well as what she looks like with add-on content. She only has 1 extra helmet and I have not purchased it yet, but I do show what it looks like. I also show off the new "menu" from this update. Instead of loading up the main menu your warframe spawns in their own ship. Levels have been greatly modified and graphics look more crisp and beautiful than ever. The pause screen has also been greatly modified. I love the new layout EXCEPT I have not found a way to switch to solo mode. I only play online when I have buddies to play with because strangers just run off and ignore other players.

Sorry for no commentary, my mic is having issues today and I wanted to get this out asap.
