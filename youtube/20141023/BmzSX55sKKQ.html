Aus der gleichen Aufnahmesession wie "Spill The Wine" stammt diese Coverversion des Rolling Stones Titels "Paint It Black" von Ex-Animal Eric Burdon und der amerikanischen Funk-Band War (26.09.1970.)

You really like Eric Burdon? You'll get him! Recorded for the Beat Club at the same session as "Spill The Wine" (September 26th, 1970) this is an improvisational cover of the old Rollig Stones classic "Paint It Black". Or is this a medley containing some other songs? We don't really know. Maybe you can help with your comments if you want! Your myBeaclub-Team.
