Hi Everyone! Just wanted to share with you 5 cards that I created with Gorjuss stamped images and WOC products that I used to embellish them. You'll will see my first take on the pop out box card, center step card, pop up card and my 2nd easel card as well as a slider card.

Pop Up Card
DCWV pastel card base/ Kaisercraft Lil Primrose
Copic skin- E000, E50, r20
Hair- Forgot =( yikes
WOC products
35mm trellis rose Pink mist FM-00131
35mm Trellis Aqua from mix green tones FM-00321
25mm open rose Dove grey (sold out) FM-00118
25mm cream open rose FM-00151
2 tone heather stem flowers FS-00269 (Bent into heart shape)
5mm and 3mm flatback self adhesive pearls

Slider card
DCWV Pastel Card base / Kaisercraft Bonjour
Copic Hair- C3-C9
Skin- E000, E50, R20
Gorjuss stamp - Santoro
WOC Products
35mm trellis pink tone mix pack FM-00511 layered on Baby Blue
Small curly Wild rose FM-00329
2 tone Cream green edge rose leaves LL-00022
Baby pink sweetheart blossoms FS-00061
2-tone baby pink/cream sweetheart blossoms FS-00063
