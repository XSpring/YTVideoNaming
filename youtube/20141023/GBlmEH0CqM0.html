Published on Aug 8, 2012

In this longer video I go through a full gruelling GBC (German Body Composition) workout. My favourite weightlifting protocol for getting lean and maintaining muscle mass (in some cases with hypertrophy too!). This video shows 2 sets of each of the 4 x supersets. 8 exercises in total, 12-15 reps target, 3 sets of each. Note the sweat and the pained expressions.... This was a real workout.... Not much time for posing (ok - well maybe just a bit) just brutality, rapid heart rate and lactic acid hell! Full squats, Dumbell Chest Press, Lunges, Chin Ups, Military Press, Hammer Curls, Unnamed Ab exercise (!!) and Tricep Pressdown....
