A peek at the redevelopment of DLF Cyber City, India's largest integrated business district. Located in Gurgaon, close to South Delhi & the International Airport, DLF Cyber City is home to a vast number of Fortune 500 companies. The focus of the animation was the planned re-development of the existing road network and creation of a rapid metro transport system.
