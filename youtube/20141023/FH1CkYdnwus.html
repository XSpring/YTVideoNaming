Published on Sep 16, 2014

9-16-14 - ( ABC - The View ) - Nicolle Wallace, who must have been resuscitated back to partial life after a coma-inducing debut as co-host of ABC’s The View, said Tuesday that President Barack Obama appears ineffective as a leader because he does not “love” people. Wallace’s comment came as she and her co-hosts were discussing conservative commentator’s Charles Krauthammer recent remark that Obama is a “narcissist.”

Wallace noted that as a former psychiatrist, Krauthammer was “offering a semi-expert opinion” on Obama and compared the president to Vice President Joe Biden who’s widely regarded as a people person.

“I think Joe Biden has an easier time with Congress because Joe Biden genuinely loves people,” Wallace said.

Rosie O’Donnell, who admitted she didn’t know who Krauthammer is, asked if Wallace was implying Obama does not “love” people.”

“Yes,” said Wallace. “I don’t think so.”

“Oh, wow,” O’Donnell replied.

“That’s bull crap,” chimed in Rosie Perez. “You are so wrong. I met him when he was a senator. He was very personable.”
